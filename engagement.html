Civic Engagement & Sustainability

Although I have not yet participated in a specific civic engagement project, I am very interested in being involved in initiatives that have a positive social or environmental impact.

I believe that being an engineer also means taking responsibility for the world we live in, and contributing to building a more sustainable and inclusive society.

In the future, I would like to participate in local environmental projects, such as trash-collecting initiatives organized by students, or engage myself to encourage more inclusion and diversity in the programming fields.

I would love to be able to use my skills in programming to have a positive impact on the world, a goal which is aligned with my wish to create positive emotions in people through digital arts.
