International Mobility

Shanghai Internship

In the summer of 2025, I had the opportunity to participate in an internship program organized by SORSA. I worked as a Vue 3 front-end developer at a small company called Kinkotech.

This was an incredibly enriching experience, and I learned a lot about working in a professional environment. I am half-french, half-chinese, so I also greatly enjoyed returning to China after almost seven years.

French Sign Language

During my first year at ENSEEIHT, I chose French Sign Language (LSF) as my second language. It is a language I find deeply interesting and fascinating. We have a fantastic and clearly passionate teacher, and the lessons are very lively and dynamic. I am very excited to continue learning LSF in the future!

UQAC

As I am very interested in video game development, I would love to spend my third year at UQAC in Quebec, as they offer an excellent video game development program. It is possible to complete a double degree, meaning I would spend a full year at the university and graduate with two diplomas.

I believe that this would give me the best of both worlds: having a general-purpose diploma with ENSEEIHT in case I wish to work as a more general-purpose programmer, and a diploma that is closer to what I want to do at UQAC.
